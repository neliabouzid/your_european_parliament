All procedures

Every single card on this page corresponds to a single legislative procedure that’s been or still is in discussion in the European Parliament. By clicking on a procedure, you will find access to the summary of its main legislative texts (i.e. the proposal and the final act) to help understand what laws are being acted in the EP. Other information, such as the key events and dates or main people involved in those procedures are also available.

The summaries of the procedures are AI-made. You can read more on how this project has been made in the About. If you see any incorrect information, please contact me.
