Capital Requirements Regulation (CRR): requirements for securities financing transactions under the net stable funding ratio
Subjects: Securities and financial markets, stock exchange, CIUTS, investments Banks and credit Financial supervision
Stage reached: Procedure completed
Reference: 2025/0077(COD)
Position Summary
The European Parliament has adopted a position to maintain current funding requirements for bank transactions involving securities financing and short-term unsecured transactions, primarily backed by sovereign debt, to ensure financial stability. This decision aims to prevent potential negative impacts on market liquidity and the costs for member states, which could affect monetary policy. The European Banking Authority (EBA) will monitor the stable funding required for these transactions and report on the appropriateness of these requirements by 2029, with subsequent reports every five years. The focus will be on assessing the fairness of funding treatments and the impact on transaction prices and market liquidity. These measures are intended to maintain a level playing field with international markets and avoid legal uncertainties, with the regulation taking effect on June 29, 2025.
