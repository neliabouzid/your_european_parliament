Establishing the Single Market and Customs Programme for the period 2028-2034
Subjects: Customs union, tax and duty-free, Community transit; Industrial competitiveness; Small and medium-sized enterprises (SME), craft industries; Customs cooperation
Stage reached: Awaiting committee decision
Reference: 2025/0590(COD)

Commission's Proposal Summary

The European Commission has proposed a new regulation to establish the Single Market and Customs Programme for the period 2028-2034, aiming to enhance the functioning of the Single Market and Customs Union. This initiative consolidates four existing funding programs into a single, streamlined effort, focusing on improving the movement of goods, services, people, and capital across the EU. The proposal seeks to simplify access to funding, reduce administrative burdens, and enhance adaptability to unforeseen needs, particularly in today's uncertain geopolitical climate. Key principles upheld by the program include 'do no significant harm' and gender equality, with provisions for preparedness and climate resilience. It also includes rules for monitoring and reporting on performance and supports overseas countries and territories linked to EU Member States, ensuring their eligibility for funding. The proposal emphasizes the need for more action to address barriers, stimulate reforms, and reduce fragmentation, especially in light of new global challenges. The program has specific objectives, such as empowering citizens, consumers, investors, economic operators, and businesses by providing information, guidance, and advice to make informed decisions and fully access the opportunities of the Single Market. It aims to foster cooperation among Member States' national authorities and between these authorities, the Commission, and other Union bodies. The program will support the design, implementation, and enforcement of Union legislation underpinning the Single Market, customs, and tax policies. A significant component of the proposal is the development and integration of European electronic systems for customs and taxation.
