Insolvency proceedings: replacing Annexes A and B
Subjects: Company law; Financial management of undertakings, business loans, accounting; Judicial cooperation in civil and commercial matters
Stage reached: Procedure completed
Reference: 2025/0023(COD)

Final Act Summary

The European Parliament and the Council have adopted a regulation to update the list of insolvency proceedings and insolvency practitioners recognized across the EU. This change, effective from October 2025, updates Annexes A and B of the existing Regulation (EU) 2015/848, which governs insolvency proceedings within the EU. The update reflects recent changes in national laws from several EU member states, including Slovakia, Estonia, Spain, Italy, Belgium, Malta, Luxembourg, Bulgaria, Czechia, and France, which have introduced new types of insolvency proceedings and practitioners. These updates ensure that the regulation remains current and applicable across the EU. Notably, Ireland has opted to participate in this regulation, while Denmark remains outside its scope. The regulation will be directly applicable in all participating EU member states, ensuring a harmonized approach to insolvency proceedings across the region.
